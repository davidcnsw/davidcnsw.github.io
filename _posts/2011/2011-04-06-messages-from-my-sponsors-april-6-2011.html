ScreenSteps Desktop - Clearer Documentation in Less Time

ScreenSteps Desktop is the simplest and fastest way to create clearer software documentation in less time. By integrating screen capture, image editing and document authoring into a single application, ScreenSteps lets you create crystal clear how-to documents in minutes instead of hours.  The documents you create can be published to services such as MindTouch, Confluence, WordPress, Joomla, Drupal and many other wiki, blog and content management systems.

  • Capture images using our integrated screen capture tools. As you capture images, your “how-to” document is assembled for you.
  • Annotate your images with arrows, boxes, circles and text boxes. Blur out sensitive information in screen captures.
  • Author text right alongside your images (no more pasting screenshots into Microsoft Word!).
  • Export documents to PDF, HTML, Word, and many blog, wiki and content management systems.
  • Add Workgroup capabilities to improve collaboration on your documentation team.
